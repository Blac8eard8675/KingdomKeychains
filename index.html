About Me

Hello and welcome to Kingdom Keychains, my name is Victoria Schwab and I am the founder of Kingdom Keychains.

My Keychains are handmade with lanyard string in a homemade spiral design.

I have Crosses that represent how God is our Anchor, next are Ropes that represent how Jesus Christ is our connection to God, and last but not least are the Chain links and they represent the Disciples and how not only are they our connection to Jesus Christ, our Lord and Savior, but also to our God, our Father in heaven.

The Crosses are priced at $10, the Ropes are $5 and the Chain links are $5 as well.

If you would like to speak with me, please feel free to message me at Victoria Schwab on Facebook Messenger or even on here at Kingdom Keychains.

I hope you enjoy what you see and once again welcome you to Kingdom Keychains.

Thank You for your support.
